## Project Name
EasyLend

## One-Line Description
Community Lending Pool with Social Credit leveraging Soroban for decentralized trust.

## Track
Track 2: Financial Inclusion & Everyday Payments

## Problem It Solves
Pure collateral-based DeFi excludes the poor who lack upfront capital. EasyLend solves this by capturing social trust on-chain, allowing community members to vouch for borrowers. This creates a path to credit for the unbanked based on reputation rather than just assets.

## How It Uses Stellar
EasyLend uses Soroban smart contracts to manage the lending pool and vouching logic. It leverages Stellar accounts for identity and Stellar assets for efficient, low-cost value transfer, ensuring micro-loans are accessible and scalable.

## Novelty Note (optional, for bonus points)
EasyLend digitizes traditional community lending models (like the "paluwagan" in the Philippines) by adding a programmable slashing mechanism for vouchers, creating a hybrid model of social and financial collateral that doesn't exist in traditional DeFi.
